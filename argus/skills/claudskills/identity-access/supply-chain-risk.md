# supply-chain-risk


## Description
Assess supply chain risk exposure and resilience posture. Analyzes supplier dependency mapping (Tier 1/2/3), geographic concentration risk, single-source vulnerability, disruption scenario modeling (pandemic, trade war, port closure), Monte Carlo simulation, financial health monitoring (Altman Z-score), early warning systems, compliance tracking (UFLPA, conflict minerals, sanctions), ESG supply chain scoring, and mitigation strategy evaluation.
