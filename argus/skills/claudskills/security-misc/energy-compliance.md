# energy-compliance


## Description
Audit energy utility software for NERC CIP cybersecurity, FERC market and tariff compliance, EPA emissions and CEMS reporting, renewable portfolio standards (RPS/REC tracking), pipeline safety (49 CFR 192/195), SCADA security, carbon market compliance, and state PUC/ISO/RTO requirements. Use when reviewing power generation, transmission, distribution, pipeline, renewable, EV charging, or energy trading codebases.
