# vendor-management


## Description
Analyze vendor management systems for performance scorecards, third-party risk assessment, SLA enforcement, vendor rationalization, and relationship governance. Evaluates vendor tiering strategies, risk taxonomy (financial, cyber, compliance, geopolitical), continuous monitoring with BitSight/SecurityScorecard, ITIL supplier management alignment, diversity program tracking, and lifecycle management from onboarding through offboarding following OCC, FFIEC, and GDPR frameworks.
